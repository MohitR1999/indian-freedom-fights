Indian Freedom Fights

A Brief History of India

India has always known a rich, cultural, diversified history. In the ancient times, most of the world's wealth was concentrated in India. Famous raiders like Mohammad Ghori, Mahmud of Ghazni invaded India and took away its wealth. Then came the Portugese, Dutch, French and the British for trade in modern period of history. The British conquered India, exploited it recklessly and drained its financial resources. The Fight for Freedom was developed against the British, in which the Indians succeeded and have created a history.
